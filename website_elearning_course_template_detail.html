Course Overview

Medical statistics is a subdiscipline of statistics. "It is the science of summarizing, collecting, presenting and interpreting data in medical practice, and using them to estimate the magnitude of associations and test hypotheses. Understanding medical statistics enable you to making sense on data obtained in medical research setting.
